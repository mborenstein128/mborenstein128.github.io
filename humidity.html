Humidity

There doesn't seem to be a strong correlation between humidity and proximity to the equator, although a large number of sampled cities that currently have 30% humidity or below seem to fall in between 20 and 50 degrees north latitude. But the higher humidity scores are spread throughout the range of latitudes, so there really isn't much conclusive that can be obtained from the data.
